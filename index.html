StackWizard Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Data Storage

StackWizard stores all session data locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on any server.

GTO Wizard

The GTO Wizard feature sends hand descriptions you type to Anthropic's API for AI analysis. This data is processed by Anthropic and is subject to their privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy. We do not store or share this data.

Analytics & Advertising

StackWizard does not use any analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking services.

Subscriptions

Subscription payments are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not store any payment or billing information.
